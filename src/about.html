About DreamCatcher

DreamCatcher is an innovative chatbot powered by artificial intelligence designed to analyze and interpret user-inputted dreams. Users can recount their dreams through text, allowing DreamCatcher to reconstruct and adapt its analyses based on user profiles, which may include optional details like age, gender, region, race/ethnicity, and zodiac. Utilizing an AI image generator, DreamCatcher creates vivid dream representations, while its analysis incorporates dream symbolism datasets and APIs, providing a personalized and comprehensive interpretation. Additionally, we envision integrating DreamCatcher into a morning assistant chatbot app, offering daily recommendations based on weather, schedule, and the user's mood or state of mind derived from their morning dream input.
